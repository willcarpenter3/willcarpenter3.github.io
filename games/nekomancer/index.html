Will Carpenter | Oct 2, 2022 min read

Description

A game made for the 2022 Red Storm and VGDC @Â NCSU game jam, where the theme was zombie cats.

You are a cat who was raised from the dead, and now you have to search for ingredients to make a cure for your zombification. Along the way you will have to make potions to transform you and use different abilities to find the correct ingredients.
